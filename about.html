The Lindsay's

Our reputation for quality furniture and service at fair prices began in 1938 when John B. Lindsay embarked on his furniture career. His son John Lindsay II, and grandsons, Benji and Ryan, carry on his legacy totaling more than 140 years of home decorating experience. Lindsay Furniture Company cares about their customers and treats each one as if they were neighbors. When you shop at Lindsay Furniture you're not dealing with a faceless corporation, but a family that's committed to doing business the right way.
